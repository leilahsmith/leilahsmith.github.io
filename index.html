ABOUT

 

Hello, I'm Leilah. I have always been a curious spirit who loves challenging myself to learn new things. Whether it be a new piece of music, knitting, or fixing my own car. My mother instilled in me the knowledge that learning is a lifelong journey, from a very young age and taught me that curiosity is the root of learning. As a musician since the age of six, I know the incredible value of the arts and how they certainly impact our world, but I always wished there were more tangible skills I could hone to make this world a better place.

When the pandemic hit and live music ceased, I knew it was time to seek new adventures that would challenge me in new ways. I wanted to make the best of a disaster and pivot towards more distinct and direct ways of helping. Such as developing technology to help eliminate problems and hurdles.
I have always had a natural love for and logical understanding of technology, so when the opportunity came to dig into software development, I knew it was right. As a professional musician I am trained to collaborate, communicate, find patterns, break big problems down into smaller chunks, and to seek out the connections, as well as a great deal of other transferable skills.
I am resilient, resourceful and love learning new things both on my own and from those who have gone on the path before me.

As a self-employed business owner since 2015, I wear many hats besides "educator" and "musician". These include administrator, accountant, marketing, content creator, audio engineer, video production, researcher, and more. I had to learn all these skills on my own through many hours of self-study and trial-and-error.

I am excited to continue my software development journey and to continue building my tech skills for the rest of my life.
